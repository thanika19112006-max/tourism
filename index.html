Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal. Summers suit hill stations; monsoons bring lush greenery to Kerala.

💉
Health & Safety

Consult your doctor for recommended vaccines. Carry a basic medical kit and travel insurance.

🚆
Getting Around

India Rail connects every major city. Book ahead on IRCTC for Rajdhani and Shatabdi express trains.

Budget Planning

India suits all budgets — from ₹800/night guesthouses to luxurious palace hotels at ₹25,000+.
